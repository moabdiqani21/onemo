FAQ

Common Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Orders are dispatched from Istanbul, Türkiye within 2–3 business days.

How do I know my size?

All our products run true to size unless stated otherwise. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for an oversized brand fit. Contact us for sizing advice on specific pieces.

How do I collaborate with ONEMO?

We love collaborating with artists, photographers, musicians, and creatives. Fill out the contact form above and select "Collaboration" as your subject. Tell us about your vision!

Do you offer wholesale or bulk orders?

Yes, we accept wholesale inquiries for select products. Please contact us via the form or email us at Onemoclu6@gmail.com with "Wholesale" in the subject line.

How can I stay updated on new drops?

Follow us on Instagram @onemo and subscribe to our newsletter using the form at the bottom of any page. Subscribers get early access to drops before anyone else.
